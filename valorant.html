Valorant Agents

WE ARE VALORANT

DEFY THE LIMITS


Phoenix

Phoenix is a self-sustaining duelist with fire-based abilities. He can heal himself and create fiery explosions to control the battlefield.

Clove

Scottish troublemaker Clove makes mischief for enemies in both the heat of combat and the cold of death. The young immortal keeps foes guessing, even from beyond the grave, their return to the living only ever a moment away.

Brimstone

Brimstone is a powerful controller who brings massive area-of-effect damage and control to any firefight. His arsenal is built to punish adversaries and secure ground.

Omen

Omen is a mysterious controller who thrives in the shadows. He can teleport across the map, blind enemies, and block vision with his abilities.

Viper

Viper is a toxic controller who uses chemical warfare to control the battlefield. She can create poisonous gas clouds and deploy a wall of toxic gas.

Reyna

Reyna is a self-sustaining duelist who consumes her enemies' essence to gain strength. She can become invulnerable, heal herself, and dismiss from danger.

Cypher

Cypher is a sentinel who gathers information and locks down areas with his spycam and tripwires. He excels at cutting off enemy advances.

Killjoy

Killjoy is a sentinel who specializes in defense and area control. She can deploy bots to damage and suppress enemies, as well as lockdown areas with her ultimate.

Skye

Skye is an initiator and healer who uses her abilities to hunt down enemies and support her team. She can summon a healing trinket and unleash a pack of wolves to hunt down foes.

Astra

Astra is a controller who manipulates the battlefield using cosmic energy. She can place stars that turn into smoke, gravity wells, or even split the map with a powerful cosmic divide.

Jett

Jett is an agile duelist who excels in mobility and precision. She uses her abilities to control engagements and outmaneuver opponents.

Yoru

Yoru is a duelist who can create gaps in enemy defenses and sneak behind enemy lines using his abilities. He can teleport, create fake footsteps, and blind enemies with a flash.
